Bridging the Divide


Education and the internet have the potential to be global equalizers.


Right now, they often widen the chasm between haves and have-nots.


Our mission is to connect the disconnected, and help make universal education truly universal.

Guiding Technological Principles
  • Cross-platform: Windows, Linux, OSX, Android
  • Easy for anyone to install
  • Synchronization; eventual consistency
  • Support cheap, low-power hardware
What does it include?
  • Django app to render lite versions of core site pages: topic pages, videos, exercises, knowledge maps.
  • Interfaces for synchronizing content (videos, exercises, thumbnails, topic tree) from the main site.
  • Student and teacher accounts, tracking progress (points!) on videos and exercises, basic coach reports.
  • Crypto-signed database records to ensure source and integrity during sync with central server.
What's coming?
  • Installation bundles for Windows, OSX, Raspberry Pi, Ubuntu/Debian.
  • Full internationalization (i18n) and support for dubbed videos.
  • Easy P2P synchronization between devices: internet, intranet, usb stick.
